Founder

Late Tarsem Singh

Founder

The visionary behind T.S ISPAT Private Limited, Shri Tarsem Singh established the company in 1986 with a dream to revolutionize the rolling mill industry in India. His engineering expertise and business acumen laid the foundation for what has become one of the country's most respected manufacturing companies.

His legacy of innovation, quality, and integrity continues to guide our company's philosophy and operations.

Chairman

Narinder Pal Singh

Chairman

With over 30 years of experience in the steel and manufacturing industry, Narinder Pal Singh has led T.S ISPAT to new heights since taking over as Chairman. Under his leadership, the company has expanded its product portfolio and international presence.
