Our Story

Founded in 1986 by Late Shri Tarsem Singh, T.S ISPAT Private Limited began as a small workshop with big dreams. Through sheer determination and engineering excellence, the company grew into one of India's most trusted names in rolling mill manufacturing.

The legacy continues under the leadership of Chairman Narinder Pal Singh and Director Jasdeep Kaur, who have expanded the company's footprint both domestically and internationally.
